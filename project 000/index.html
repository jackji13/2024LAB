DOXER - A cautionary platform that delves into the implications of personal data exposure in the digital era. Inspired by vibrant discussions on Hacker News about avatar generation and fake information for scams, Doxer transforms personal data such as names, ages, and addresses into engaging visual narratives. This website artfully raises awareness about the ease with which our digital identities can be leaked and misused, urging a reflection on data privacy. Through interactive displays and stark visualizations, Doxer not only highlights the vulnerabilities inherent in digital interactions but also encourages more guarded and informed behaviors online, making it a compelling resource for those concerned with safeguarding their personal information.
